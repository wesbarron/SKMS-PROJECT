What are Zero-Day Attacks?

The term "Zero-Day" refers to the short amount of time that a corporation has to fix the flaw that led to the attack. The term infers a high level attack that is crucial for the organization to both prevent from happening while also having a plan of action when an attack occurs. Corporations take these attacks very seriously.
